# Data Sources

Source data are not redistributed. All inputs are available to approved
researchers through PPMI.

**Access:** Free registration and data use agreement at
[ppmi-info.org](https://www.ppmi-info.org) (RRID: SCR_006431).

**Data vintage:** Last accessed via the PPMI portal on June 24, 2024.
Data collected between July 7, 2010 and May 24, 2024.

---

## Required variables

**Demographics.** Age at baseline, sex, self-reported race (two levels),
ethnicity (two levels), education (three levels), years since original
diagnosis at baseline, family history of PD.

**Genotyping.** Genome-wide sequencing from PPMI Genetics Core
(Illumina, GATK Best Practices). Participants classified as sporadic PD,
*LRRK2* PD (G2019S, R1441C/G + M1646T, N2081D/N14D), or *GBA* PD
(E326K, R502C, A495P, N409S). Exclusions: *SNCA*, *PRKN*, *PINK1*,
*PARK7*, *VPS35* carriers; dual *LRRK2*+*GBA* carriers; SWEDD.

**α-Synuclein SAA.** Baseline CSF α-syn SAA results (positive/negative)
from 24-h (n=59) and 150-h (n=505) Amprion protocols. Kinetic parameters
for 150-h protocol: time to threshold (TTT), time to 50% threshold
(T50), fluorescence maximum (Fmax), and area under the curve (AUC),
averaged across replicates.

**Motor outcomes.** MDS-UPDRS Part III (OFF-state), 7-year follow-up
(mean 5.81 years). LEDD at each visit.

**Non-motor outcomes.** MDS-UPDRS Part I (non-motor experiences of daily
living), 7-year follow-up.

**Cognitive outcomes.** Montreal Cognitive Assessment (MoCA), 7-year
follow-up.

**Dopaminergic imaging.** DAT-SPECT specific binding ratios (caudate and
putamen), 5-year follow-up (mean 3.98 years).
